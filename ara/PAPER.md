# CheckEval-Guided Preference Evaluation

This Agent-Native Research Artifact records the research provenance for the
CheckEval-guided selector and dynamic-evaluation thesis workflow.

## Layers

- `logic/`: problem framing, claims, concepts, experiments, and solution notes.
- `trace/`: exploration tree and session-level provenance.
- `evidence/`: raw or summarized experimental evidence.
- `staging/`: observations not yet promoted into claims or heuristics.

## Current Focus

The active research thread asks whether a learned selector can choose a small
set of human-relevance checklist questions that preserves or improves pairwise
evaluation accuracy relative to evaluating the full frozen checklist bank.
